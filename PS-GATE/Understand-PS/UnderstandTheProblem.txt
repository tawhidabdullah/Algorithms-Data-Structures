//////////////// when i am about to solve a problem this question i should asked to myself /////////////////

1. Can i restate the problem in my own words/way ?

2. What are the input that go into the problem ?

3. What are the outputs that should come from the solution for the problem ?

4. Can the outputs be determined from the inputs ? 
    in other words :> do i have enough information to solve the problem (i may not be able to answer the question
    untill i start to solve the problem) but it's still worth to asked myself this question at this early stage.

5. How should i label the important pieces of data that are part of problem ?
